Unveiling a New Era of Accessibility for the Visually Impaired

At Visionary Assist, we specialize in creating innovative smart spectacles for the visually impaired, empowering them with greater independence and accessibility. Our product features intelligent navigation, facial recognition, and text-to-speech capabilities, enhancing mobility, social interaction, and literary access. We're committed to transforming the lives of those with visual impairments, ensuring safety, ease of use, and a richer, more connected experience. Join us in redefining the boundaries of possibility with Visionary Assist – where every step is confident, every interaction meaningful, and every word accessible.

Application of VISIONARY 1.0

  • CITY NAVIGATION: Assist blind individuals in navigating through urban environments, including streets, sidewalks, and public spaces.

  • INDOOR NAVIGATION: Help users navigate through indoor spaces like shopping malls, airports, and public buildings, providing information about nearby stores, facilities, and services.

  • READING ASSISTANCE: Aid in reading printed text, such as signs, labels, and documents, through text-to-speech functionality.

  • PERSONALIZED EXPERIENCES: Provide personalized experiences based on user preferences, such as preferred routes, locations, and types of information.
